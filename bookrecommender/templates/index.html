Welcome to The Book Spot!

Thanks for checking out my book recommender! This small app will ask you to create an account, then rate several books you've already read. The app will then throw your rating information into a big matrix and perform collaborative based filtering (specifically matrix factorization) to recommend new books to you that you may like. This is, in broad strokes, how companies like Amazon and Netflix recommend new items or movies to users.

What do I do?

To start, just register for an account. Then start searching for books you've read before and go ahead and give them ratings. To improve your recommendations, try to rate more books that you enjoyed than that you didn't. Once you've rated at least five books (the more the better), the algorithm will throw you into the matrix and compute your recommendations. This happens once a day; so while you wait, check out the popular books to see if you're interested in those!
